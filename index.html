What The Game Is

Battles are designed around steady gold gain, active skill timing, party-building, and permanent upgrades that carry across the campaign. Players recruit heroes, strengthen their core lineup, and push into harder stages as their roster grows.

Core Features

  • Fantasy idle battler combat built for mobile
  • Hero recruiting through the Codex
  • Permanent account-wide upgrades
  • Battle actions like Focus Burst, Flask, stun, and debuffs
  • Gold-based progression and long-form stage pushing

The Journey

Starting in Emberwood and pushing deeper into a world shaped by the Calamity, players build momentum one battle at a time. Every new recruit, every upgrade, and every clear feeds into a stronger, faster, more dangerous party.

Built For Progression

Embershard Quest is focused on the feeling of growth. More gold means more upgrades. More heroes means more team strength. Longer fights create space for better decisions, stronger builds, and a more satisfying climb through the campaign.
